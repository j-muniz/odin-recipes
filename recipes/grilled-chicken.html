Grilled Chicken

Perfect golden chicken wings

I might have a more hands on approach when it comes to grilling, but it sure is worth it when you get to bite down on perfectly golden brown crispy wings seasoned to perfection. Give it a try!

It all started on a cool autumn afternoon back in 1995. The leaves were falling from the trees and blowing in the wind. My father would often cook on the grill. The aroma would fill the air and make my young tummy rumble with anticipation. This is when I knew I would forever be entranced from these wonderful smells coming from that hot box of fire that I was to not touch or go near....(Three pages later)... alas I will at last divulge you some of my preferred spices and cooking method for this classic and simple meal.

Ingredients

Please feel free to substitute any of the ingredients to your liking.

  • chicken wings(or any part of the chicken you like)
  • olive oil
  • Badia: all perpose seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • choped cilantro(optional)

Steps

When it comes to seasoning I eye ball it, and let my ancestors tell me when I have put enough. You must FEEL it in your SOUL!!

Food prep

  1. rinse the chicken(if you'd like)
  2. place chicken in mixing bowl
  3. drizzle enough oil to coat the wings to let the dry rub stick better to the meat
  4. generously cover the wings with the Badia all purpose seasoning
  5. there can never be too much garlic in my house hold, so sprinkle to taste(or don't its your food)
  6. salt to taste
  7. for extra flavor boost mix in chopped cilantro
  8. let sit for at least 15min
  9. if using chicken breast add a pinch of baking soda to keep it tender and juicy

Cooking instructions

cook time ~20-25min

  1. If using a propane grill set on low heat
  2. to keep track of what wings have been flipped; keep all wing on same sides, and flip every 2-5min(to prevent burning)
  3. when wings are golden perfection remove from heat and enjoy
