Instant Pot Baby Back Ribs

description heading

description

Ingredients

  • Ribs
  • olive oil
  • dry rub of choice
  • bbq sauce
  • apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. clean ribs and cut away as much fat as possible and remove that membrane (optional)
  2. drizzle some olive oil onto the ribs
  3. generously coat and rub in the dry rub (let it sit for 15min or so, optional)
  4. place the metal rack known as a trivet into the instant pot
  5. pour a little bit of water and the vinegar to about a quarter-half of the legs of the rack
  6. place ribs in and cook for about 30min on high pressure
  7. let the meat naturally decompress (you could seize the meat making it tougher)
  8. place it onto a backing pan and brush on your choice of bbq sauce
  9. place into oven set to broil for 5-10min to let the bbq sauce caramelize
  10. remove to let it rest and enjoy a super tender fall off the bone rack of ribs
